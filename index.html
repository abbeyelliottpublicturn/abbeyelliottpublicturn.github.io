Social Media: How It Negatively Affects Your Mental Health

Problematic Social Media Use

Individuals that experience problematic social media use have an extreme desire to be on social media. These characteristics of this condition can cause addiction and symptoms of depression.

Addiction to Social Media

Addiction to social media is very possible through many of the features that social media platforms utilize. These include likes, followers, comments, and other interactive features that keep users looking at their notifications.

Increased Symptoms of Depression

Through research, it has been shown that symptoms of depression can be increased significantly through negative experiences that users might have on social media use.

Generational Differences in Social Media Use

Through research, it has been shown that many younger generations have higher rates in social media use and display more mental illnesses. It has been called into question whether social media is causing this increase.
