Problematic Social Media Use

Problematic Social Media Use can be characterized by many things. Individuals that experience problematic social media use have an extreme desire to be on social media. If an individual dedicates copious amounts of time to social media that it limits other activities, they exhibit problematic social media use. These characteristics of this condition can cause addiction and symptoms of depression.

Addiction to Social Media

Addiction to social media can look different in every individual. However, addiction is very possible. Symptoms of addiction include: being overly concerned about social media, having an uncontrollable urge to log onto the websites, and spending large amounts of time on the platforms. If the time spent on social media is shortened, those with addictions can become irritable, and the time usually spent on social media negatively affects other areas in life of these individuals.
